Steel Magnolias

Steel Magnolias tells the bittersweet tale of six women in a small Louisiana town whose lives intersect at the local beauty parlour. The story deals with the changes - some of them painful and poignant, some of them hilarious - that the women experience over the course of several years. Based on Robert Harling's award-winning play. Presented by Canterbury Theatre Guild.
