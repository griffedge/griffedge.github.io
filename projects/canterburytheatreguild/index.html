Canterbury Theatre Guild

The Canterbury Theatre Guild is a not-for-profit community based musical theatre group where members of the community are welcome to join and participate in putting on a musical, concert and/or cabaret once or twice a year.

  • Website designed and built by Griffen Edge using Wix.

I am currently the Marketing Manager at Canterbury Theatre Guild, so I am responsible for promoting our events to the community. I have designed a wide range of promotional material for the guild, ranging from tickets to programs, digital advertising, videos and the website.
