Canterbury Theatre Guild

Canterbury Theatre Guild is a not-for-profit community based musical theatre group where members of the community are welcome to join and participate in putting on a musical, concert and/or cabaret once or twice a year.

I was the Marketing Manager at Canterbury Theatre Guild until April 2019 and was responsible for promoting our events to the community. I designed a wide range of promotional material for the guild, ranging from tickets to programs, digital advertising, videos and the website.
