Pledge & Protect

Using smart technology to make riding safely easy.

Live prototype

Type

Project for DECO3200: Interactive Product Design Studio at the University of Sydney

Duration

August to November 2020

Role

I was on a team of 3 (myself, Nancy Nguyen, and Raunak Sitoula). My responsibilities included research, ideation, user testing, video production, and interface design.

Summary

Pledge & Protect is a community mapping of loved ones that need to be protected from COVID-related complications and people who pledge to practice physical distancing and other COVID-safe behaviours.
