About me

Griffen Edge is an aspiring designer who is currently studying a Bachelor of Design Computing at the University of Sydney. He believes that good design has the power to make the world a better place.

Griffen is the former President of Sydney University Experience Designers [SUEDE], a society driven by students passionate about the design of everyday interactions, services, and experiences. He is the former Marketing Manager at Canterbury Theatre Guild, where he promoted the performing arts to the community. He has contributed to a number of Github repositories including Prioritab, which he redesigned and is now used by over 18,000 users.

He has always had an interest in design, be it with photography, stage lighting, fashion, interior design or digital media.
