About

Griffen Edge is an aspiring designer who finished the HSC in 2017 at Sydney Secondary College Blackwattle Bay Campus. He believes that good design has the power to make the world a better place.

Griffen is currently the Marketing Manager at Canterbury Theatre Guild, where he promotes the performing arts to the community. He has contributed to a number of Github repositories including Prioritab, which he redesigned and is now used by over 18,000 users.
