About Me

Hey there! I’m Griffen, a user experience designer currently studying Design Computing at the University of Sydney. I believe that good design has the power to make the world a better place.

I’m the former President and Vice President of Sydney University Experience Designers [SUEDE], a society driven by students passionate about the design of everyday interactions, services, and experiences. I am the former Marketing Manager at Canterbury Theatre Guild, where I promoted the performing arts to the community. I have contributed to a number of Github repositories including Prioritab, which I redesigned and is now used by over 18,000 users.

I have always had an interest in design, be it with photography, stage lighting, fashion, interior design or digital media.
